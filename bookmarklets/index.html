Welcome to my collection of bookmarklets.

Most of these weren't created by me, so if you see a bookmarklet you made and want credit, please join the Discord and create a ticket.


Steps on how to add bookmarklets:

Step 1. Click on one of the buttons shown below, it will automatically copy the code to your clipboard.

Step 2. Right click on your bookmarks bar.

Step 3. Select "Add page..." in the right click menu.

Step 4. In the name text box, name it whatever you want. In the URL box, paste the code that was copied to your clipboard.

Now, you've finished! On any website, click the bookmark like a normal bookmark, and your bookmarklet should work!
